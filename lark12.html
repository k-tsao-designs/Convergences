Then they both left the castle They flew away as far as they could from the castle that lay east of the sun and west of the moon. secretly in the night, for they feared the princess's father, who was a sorcerer.

They mounted the griffin, which carried them across the Red Sea, and when they were halfway, she dropped the nut. Immediately a tall nut tree grew up, and the bird rested on it, and then carried them home.

There they found their child, who had grown tall and handsome, and from that time they lived happily until they died.
